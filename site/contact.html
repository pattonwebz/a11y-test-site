Meet our team

Our small but mighty crew bakes fresh every morning, sources ingredients from local farms, and makes sure every customer leaves with a smile (and a full bag).

Founded in 2026, Demo Bakery started as a weekend pop-up at the farmers' market and grew into a neighbourhood staple — proof that good bread brings people together.
